UC Courses

BYU Independent Study high school courses are accredited by the Northwest Accreditation Commission (NWAC), a division of AdvancED.

Our complete portfolio of electives is available to ALL high school students, UC college-bound and otherwise.

Each course meets the California 1-semester (5-credit) requirement.

If students plan to attend a non-UC school, please check with the school about admission requirements for high school courses.

To see the most up-to-date list of our UC-Approved courses, view our UC A-G Course List at http://hs-articulation.ucop.edu/agcourselist#/list/details/4241/.
