UC Courses

BYU Independent Study high school courses are accredited by the Northwest Accreditation Commission (NWA), a division of AdvancED.

Our complete portfolio of electives is available to ALL high school students, UC college-bound and otherwise.

If students plan to attend a non-UC school, please check with the school about admission requirements for high school courses.

To see another list of our UC-Approved courses, visit https://hs-articulation.ucop.edu and search for "BYU" under High Schools.
